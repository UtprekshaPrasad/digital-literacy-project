# Stay safe online:
1. Use strong passwords: Create unique passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. 
2. Avoid using the same password across multiple accounts.
3. Enable 2-factor authentication (2FA): Always turn on 2FA for email, banking, and social media accounts for extra security.
4. Avoid suspicious links & emails: Do not click on unknown links or download attachments from unverified sources—these may contain malware or ransomware.
5. Keep software updated: Regularly update your phone, laptop, and apps to fix security vulnerabilities.
6. Install antivirus software: Use trusted antivirus tools to detect and prevent malware attacks.
7. Never share OTP or UPI PIN: Banks or apps will never ask for your OTP or PIN—keep it confidential at all times.
8. Verify payment requests carefully: Always check the receiver’s name before approving any UPI payment. Avoid “collect request” scams.
9. Use official apps only: Download banking or payment apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.
10. Report Cybercrime:
    -Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal(cybercrime.gov.in)
    -Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
